Leading practices rely on Accountable Inpatient Medicine

AIM is a hospitalist group that serves primary care physicians. By utilizing our free of cost hospitalist services, PCP's can save time, increase their outpatient volume, all while maintaining open lines of communication with our inpatient team. AIM advocates for their clients in the inpatient setting. We do not have an outpatient practice, guaranteeing PCP patient retention upon hospital discharge.
